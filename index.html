Privacy Policy

Family Budget App  ·  Effective date: 2026-05-07

This Privacy Policy applies to the Family Budget application (the "Application") for mobile devices, developed by O.K. Apps (the "Service Provider") as a free service. The Application is provided "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information solely to provide its core features: real-time budget synchronization, expense tracking, and shared family budget management.

Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Your name and email address, used for authentication via Firebase.
  • Financial Records: Income, expenses, category names, notes, budget limits, savings goals, and recurring payments that you manually enter.
  • Receipt Photos: Photos of receipts you choose to upload. These are stored securely in Firebase Storage and used solely as visual attachments. They are not processed by AI, OCR, or any automated analysis.
  • Shared Budget Data: When you use the Shared Budget feature, all your financial records and uploaded photos will be visible to the partner you have explicitly invited to join your budget group. Please share access only with people you trust.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Basic device information (IP address, operating system, device model) collected by Firebase for stability and error reporting.

Biometric Data

The Application supports login via Fingerprint or Face ID as a convenience feature. The Service Provider does not collect, access, or store any biometric data. All biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system (Android or iOS) and never leaves your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses the following trusted third-party services, each governed by their own Privacy Policy:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage
  • Expo

3. Data Storage and Synchronization

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (structured data) and Firebase Storage (photos) to enable real-time synchronization across devices for you and your partner.
  • Offline Mode: Data is cached locally on your device when offline and automatically synchronized with Firebase once a connection is restored.
  • Data Security: All data is encrypted in transit between the Application and Firebase servers using industry-standard TLS protocols. Access to family data is protected by Firebase Security Rules, ensuring that only authorized family members can read or modify it.

4. Account and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and can request deletion at any time.

  • In-App Deletion (Budget Owner): If you created the family budget, using the "Delete Account" option in Settings will permanently delete your profile, all family financial records, receipt photos, goals, limits, and recurring payments from our servers. Your partner's account will be automatically unlinked but not deleted.
  • In-App Deletion (Invited Partner): If you joined another person's budget, using "Delete Account" will remove your account and revoke your access to the shared budget. The budget owner's data will not be affected.
  • Email Request: You may contact the Service Provider at o.kuro4ka@gmail.com to request data deletion. Requests will be processed within 30 days.
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the Application stops further data collection on your device but does not automatically delete your cloud data. Please use the "Delete Account" feature before uninstalling if you wish to remove all data.

5. Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be promptly deleted from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at o.kuro4ka@gmail.com.

6. Security

We take the security of your financial information seriously. The Application employs physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards — including Firebase Security Rules — to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
